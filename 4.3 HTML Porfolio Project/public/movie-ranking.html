The Best Animes According to Shahar Bar Natan

My top 3 animes of all-time.

One-Punch Man

One Punch Man stands out as a masterclass in comedic timing and animation. With its unique concept of an overpowered hero who can defeat any foe with a single punch, it playfully subverts typical superhero tropes. Beyond its humor, the anime impresses with high-quality animation that brings epic fight scenes to life. This series was ahead of its time, blending comedy, action, and a refreshing storyline that keeps viewers hooked from start to finish.

Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba

Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba redefined anime with its breathtaking animation and stunning visuals. Jujutsu Kaisen

Jujutsu Kaisen follows the story of Yuji Itadori, a high school student who stumbles into the world of cursed spirits after swallowing a powerful, cursed object to protect his friends. Now possessed by the ancient curse Sukuna, Yuji is thrust into a battle between life and death as he joins the Tokyo Jujutsu High to train as a sorcerer. With a gripping story full of dark curses, intense battles, and memorable characters, Jujutsu Kaisen masterfully blends horror, action, and humor, capturing the struggles of good versus evil in a way that feels both fresh and thrilling.
